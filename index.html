What We Do

Tunali is a professional textile screen printing company based in Wangsa Melawati, Kuala Lumpur. With a client base heavily made up of high street fashion brands through out the Malaysia all work is produced to the highest standard.

We offer a wide range of printing and embroidery techniques which can all be viewed on our services below:


  • Team Uniforms
  • Family Reunions
  • Band Shirts
  • Promotional Marketing Items
  • Special Events
  • Staff Uniforms
  • Custom T-Shirt Design
  • Embroidery

We love our customers

We love our customers. No really, we do. Just making you satisfied isn’t good enough for us, we’ll do everything in our power to ensure you’re not just satisfied but absolutely in love with your impressive new T-Shirt.

We are quality obsessed

We use high quality shirts which allow designs to be printed with an exceptional amount of clarity. Our shirts are also available in the full range of sizes, so that you can easily find a shirt which will fit and order in the quantity your desire.

100% love guarantee

If you’re not happy for any reason we’ll make it right or give you your money back. No small print here, it’s really that simple. No hassles.

We are creative
